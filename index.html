What is popular now!

The most ordered flavor we have is either the strawberry or the Oreo. The strawberry is vinella buttercream with freshy cut strawberry between each layer. The oreo is hand crushed oreos and whipped cream mixed together and put between each layer to create heaven in cake form!

How To Order A Cake

  1. Deside How much you are feeding, so you can determine a size!

  2. Depending on the amount being served you can have multiple teirs (A teir is cake/ buttercream/ cake/ buttercream/ cake).

  3. It cake is muli teired you can have different flavors see the menu for choices!

  4. Now that all of that is out of the way here is the fun part! You get to pick a theme you may send pictures of other cakes I will not copy those pictures, but I will put elements of the cakes you like into the cake.

  5. Then give some info on what the party is, ie. Age Name and what ever other details I may need!
